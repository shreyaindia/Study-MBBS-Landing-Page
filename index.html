Top MBBS Destinations

Russia

Tuition Fees: $3,500 - $7,000/year

Top Universities: Moscow State University, Kazan Federal University

Advantages: Low-cost education, WHO-recognized, globally accepted degree.

Uzbekistan

Tuition Fees: $3,000 - $5,000/year

Top Universities: Tashkent Medical Academy, Samarkand State Medical University

Advantages: English-medium programs, affordable living, high-quality education.

Egypt

Tuition Fees: $6,000 - $9,000/year

Top Universities: Cairo University, Ain Shams University

Advantages: Strong medical infrastructure, English-medium, globally recognized degrees.

Philippines

Tuition Fees: $3,000 - $5,500/year

Top Universities: University of Santo Tomas, Davao Medical School

Kazakhstan

Tuition Fees: $3,500 - $6,000/year

Top Universities: Al-Farabi Kazakh National University, Astana Medical University

Advantages: Low-cost education, WHO & NMC approved, good infrastructure.

Georgia

Tuition Fees: $5,000 - $8,000/year

Top Universities: Tbilisi State Medical University, Batumi Shota Rustaveli University

Advantages: European education system, high global acceptance, English-medium courses.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Age Requirement atleast 18year
  • 10th & 12th mark sheets and passing certificates
  • Birth certificate
  • Medical fitness certificate
  • English Proficiency
  • NEET Exam Requirement (For Indian Students)
  • At least 50% in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (PCB) for general category students.
  • 40% for reserved categories (SC/ST/OBC) (as per Indian NMC guidelines).
